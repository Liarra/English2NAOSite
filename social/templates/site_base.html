Welcome to English2NAO!

Hi! Welcome to English2NAO, the natural language tool for programming robots. Just type what you want the robot to do in plain formal English, and your program is ready.

Tell what your want the robot to do

Write down your requirements in the steps on the left. 

 Use words like "and" or "while" to execute two actions at once.  Type "go to step N" to make the robot do the step number N.

The robot currently can do the following tricks:
- Say something;
- Wait;
- Wave;
- Dance;
- Nod;
- Cry.

It can also sit, stand and kneel!

Check and modify your program

Check if English2NAO understood your instruction. If you see question marks signs - then it missed something!

The numbers show you in which order the instructions will be executed.

You can remove the whole row of instructions or go deep and edit its individual components.
